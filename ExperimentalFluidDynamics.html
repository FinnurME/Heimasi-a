Experimental Fluid Dynamics

DTU - 41822

Flow visualization

The course objective is to give an introduction to measuring techniques for determining velocity, flow, pressure, temperature etc. for fluids under stationary as well as nonstationary conditions. The course gives basic measurement theory and gives examples of physical interpretation of measurements. The course will give practical laboratory experience.

Official course description and other information can be found at kurser.dtu.dk.
