VÉL608M

Modern Manufactoring Processes

Ragnar, Finnur and Eldar

In this course we learn the fundimentals of digital manufacturing technology and modern manufacturing processes. The following list covers the course syllabus:

  1. Version management and website design for a web-based portfolio and documentation.
  2. Parametric design for laser cutting and computerized milling (CNC).
  3. Design in 3D for 3D-printers and 3D-scanning.
  4. Design of casting molds and the usage of relevant materials.
  5. Design of larger units for milling.
  6. Project management for developement- and innovation projects

Official course description and other information can be found at Uglan.
