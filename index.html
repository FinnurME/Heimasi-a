This website is made during the course Modern Manufacturing Processes (VÉL608M) but is currently a work in progress. It will contain information about my ongoing and finished projects.

I am currently doing my masters degree at DTU in Mechanical engineering, Industrial Fluid Mechanics. I am planning on using this website as documentation of various projects, an extension of my CV and as practice for html5. I studied mechanical engineering at the University of Iceland and graduated in 2022. The focus of my B.Sc. was mechanical design and thermodynamics. I also partook in the Formula Student team there, Team Spark, in which I mostly did CFD for the cars design of aerodynamic devices. I later became the project manager for Team Spark. I've also founded a start-up that has a patent pending. I've since sold my share in that start up for a small profit in order to pursue my interest for chaotic systems, such as turbulent flows and disc golf.
